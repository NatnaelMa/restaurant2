How Can We Help You?

Find answers to common questions, contact our team, or get support for your Hardrock Restaurant experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

Hardrock Restaurant is open 7 days a week:

  • Monday - Thursday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Friday - Saturday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Hours may vary on holidays. Please check our website or call ahead during holiday seasons.

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of all sizes. You can make a reservation:

  • Online through our website reservation system
  • By calling us directly at (555) 123-4567
  • Through our mobile app (available on iOS and Android)

We recommend making reservations for weekends and special occasions at least 48 hours in advance.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for our guests. Additionally, there is a public parking garage located just one block away from our restaurant with reasonable hourly rates.

For disabled guests, we have dedicated accessible parking spaces right in front of the restaurant entrance.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We are committed to accommodating our guests' dietary needs. Our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server about any allergies or dietary restrictions, and our chefs will be happy to prepare a meal that meets your requirements.

For severe allergies, please speak with our manager when you arrive so we can take extra precautions.

What is your cancellation policy for reservations?

We understand that plans change. If you need to cancel or modify your reservation, please let us know at least 2 hours before your scheduled time. For large parties (8+ guests), we require 24 hours notice for cancellation to avoid a $25 per person fee.

You can cancel or modify your reservation through the confirmation email you received when booking, by calling us, or through our mobile app.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Hardrock Restaurant gift cards are available in any denomination from $25 to $500. You can purchase them:

  • At the restaurant host stand
  • Online through our website
  • By phone at (555) 123-4567

Gift cards never expire and can be used for dining, takeout, or our retail merchandise.

Do you host private events or parties?

Yes, we have private dining spaces available for events ranging from intimate gatherings to large celebrations (up to 150 guests). Our event coordinators can help customize menus and arrangements for:

  • Corporate events
  • Wedding receptions
  • Birthday parties
  • Anniversaries
  • Holiday parties

For more information, please contact our events team at events@hardrockrestaurant.com or call (555) 123-4568.
